What is Formula 1?

Formula One (also known as Formula 1 or F1) is the highest class of international auto racing for single-seater racing cars sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA).
A Formula One season consists of a series of races, known as Grands Prix (French for 'grand prizes' or 'great prizes'), which take place worldwide on purpose-built circuits and on closed public roads.
After each Race players receive Championship points, the better position you are in the race, the more points, if you finish 11-20 you receive no points unless you have a fastest lap where you get 1 point.
There are also points awarded to the constructors (Teams) based on their position in the race.
